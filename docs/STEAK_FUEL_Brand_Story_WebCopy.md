## BRAND STORY (Long-form / About Page)

### Headline
> **WE DIDN'T INVENT PROTEIN. WE JUST STOPPED BURNING IT.**

### The Story

Most high-protein snacks are a lie.

The grilling. The frying. The extrusion. The spray-drying. Every shortcut that turns a fast food snack into something with a protein claim also destroys the amino acids that make protein worth eating. By the time it reaches the shelf, the Creatine is gone. The Taurine is denatured. What remains is a protein number on a label — not fuel in your body.

We built STEAK FUEL to fix that.

The method is ancient. South African butchers have been air-curing beef since the 1600s — hanging whole cuts in dry, ventilated air, letting time and salt do what heat cannot: concentrate the meat without destroying it. Three kilograms of premium raw beef becomes one kilogram of steak at its most powerful. Moisture out. Protein density in. Nothing lost.

What you get is 67 grams of protein per 100 grams. More protein than a chicken breast. More than two scoops of most whey powders. And unlike powders, you're eating *real food* — with the complete amino acid matrix intact. Creatine. Taurine. Heme-Iron. B12. Zinc. The compounds that your body actually uses to contract, recover, and grow.

We're based in Kerala, India — sourcing from local farms, slicing to spec, and air-drying to precision humidity in our production facility. Every pouch is vacuum-sealed within hours of finishing. The result tastes like what it is: premium steak, intensified.

STEAK FUEL isn't a snack. It's a recovery stack in a pouch.

## PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY SECTION

### Section Header
> **THE SCIENCE OF NOT COOKING IT**

### Body Copy

High-heat cooking above 70°C begins to denature heat-sensitive amino acids. Creatine — your primary ATP regeneration compound — starts degrading around 150°C. The average grill runs at 250°C. Your pre-workout replaces what cooking destroys.

Air-curing operates below 35°C. The entire protein structure remains intact. This is not a claim — it is chemistry.

## BRAND VOICE GUIDE

### Voice: The Coach Who Reads Labels
Speak like someone who lifts, eats clean, and has read the science — but doesn't hide behind jargon. Direct. Precise. Occasionally blunt. The brand never apologises for being the best option on the shelf.

**Say:**
- "Air-cured. Not cooked. The difference is bioavailability."
- "67g per 100g. Compare that to your current snack."
- "We're not competing with jerky. We're competing with your supplement stack."
- "Recovery starts at the counter. Not at the shaker."

**Never say:**
- "Delicious!" / "Tasty!" / "Yummy!" (Too FMCG-snack)
- "Healthy snacking" (Undersells the product category)
- "Guilt-free" (Our customer doesn't feel guilty about protein)
- "Premium quality" (Show, don't claim)
